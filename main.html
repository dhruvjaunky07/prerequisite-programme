Prerequisite Programme

🧼 Personal Hygiene

  • Wash hands regularly
  • Wear protective clothing
  • No jewelry in work areas

🧹 Cleaning & Sanitation

  • Scheduled cleaning routines
  • Use approved chemicals
  • Maintain cleaning records

🐭 Pest Control

  • Prevent entry points
  • Monitor traps regularly
  • Report infestations immediately

🗑️ Waste Management

  • Segregate waste properly
  • Timely disposal
  • Keep bins clean

🌾 Allergen Control

  • Label allergens clearly
  • Segregate allergen materials
  • Clean equipment thoroughly

🛡️ Foreign Body Prevention

  • Use sieves and filters
  • Install detectors
  • Protective covers on machinery
